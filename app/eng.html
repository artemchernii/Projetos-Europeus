SPHINX - CYBER PROTECTION OF THE HEALTH AND CARE IT
Universal cyber security tools for healthcare
Participar Saber mais
The SPHINX European Project, in the scope of Horizon 2020, aims to develop a Universal Cyber Security Toolkit, with the purpose of improving and ensuring the cybernetic protection of the IT ecosystem, in Healthcare area, in Hospitals and Care Centers.

What is the focus of SPHINX?

It will focus on privacy and data integrity and infrastructure, proactive assessment and mitigation of cybersecurity threats, evaluation and verification of the new medical services and devices, vulnerability assessment (almost in real-time) of IT operating systems, among others topics.

The solution to develop aims to:

  • Improve the security of Health and Care services, data and infrastructures;
  • Reduce the risk of data privacy breaches, caused by cyber attacks;
  • Ensure patient's trust and safety.

The solution will be easily adapted or incorporated into existing medical, clinical or health infrastructures and will be tested and validated through pan-European demonstrations in three different scenarios in Romania, Portugal and Greece.

5G MOBILIZER - COMPONENTS AND SERVICES FOR 5G NETWORKS
Operationalization and exploitation of 5G networks
Participate Lean more
Intends to create a new generation of mobile communication (new products and services) and to make 5G networks (faster, almost instantaneous) a reality for Portuguese consumers from 2020.

The Project approaches (investigating, developing and demonstrating) a set of key issues covering all of the 5G networks functional domains. A series of demonstrators will be developed, with the intention of exploring the possible applications associated with these networks.

Which solutions can demonstrators offer to users?
  • To visit a remote tourist spot through Virtual Reality glasses, with the use of drones that transmit 360º images in real time;
  • To choose how to see an event (a football game, a show or a concert, e.g.) using Virtual Reality and 360º perspectives;
  • To create a sensorial virtual experience, through image capture, allowing, for example, to virtually visit a place or perform a radical activity.

How does PDMFC act on the 5G Mobilizer Project?

At PDMFC, we are involved in the Research, Development and Production of the necessary technologies, having as focus the areas of Security, Virtual/Augmented Reality Applications, Games and Immersive Visual Experiences.

SECREDAS

Automated and reliable systems in multiple domains
The goal of SECREDAS (Product Security for Cross Domain Reliable Dependable Automated Systems) Project is to suggest and validate a modular architecture to create solutions that unsure Security, Protection and Privacy of all citizens. It aims to make connected and automated vehicles in a safe and reliable reality.
How does it intend to create Secure and Safe Automated Systems?

The technology to be developed on SECREDAS will allow monitoring individuals and their interaction with the environment. It focuses, in particular, in control and automation systems, mainly on Health, Transports and Aerospace sectors, covering technologies such as radar, vehicle-to-Infrastructure and in-vehicle networks.

How does PDMFC act on the SECREDAS Project?

PDMFC will take an important step on safe technology for connected and automated vehicles, intervening in the areas of Cybersecurity, AI, Privacy and Decisions Support Systems.

https://www.ecsel.eu/projects/secredas

AFarCloud

Aggregate Farming in the Cloud
Aims to develop a range of technological solutions for Precision Farming, with an almost total level of automation.
How will AFarCloud meet farming needs?

AFarCloud uses different technologies, such as Continuous Data Analysis, Autonomous Vehicles, Simulation Tools and Self-learning and will allow the integration and cooperation of Cyber Physical Systems in real-time to improve the quality and efficiency of farming processes. It will have Pilot Farms as demonstrators, without the need of human presence.

How does PDMFC act on the POSITION II Project?

The fields of AI, Autonomous Drones, Image Recognition and IoT are the focus of PDMFC on the Project.

https://www.ecsel.eu/projects/afarcloud

POSITION II

Next generation of smart catheters
Focuses on the development of open technology platforms for smart medical catheters that are minimally invasive.
How to prepare the European Electronic Industry for the next health field revolution?

With the POSITION II Project, platforms that will be the ideal technological structure to produce implants will be developed and open to numerous users and applications. It will allow to better diagnose using sensors and ultra-sound devices, streamline the workflow in the cath lab, among others benefits, improving the quality of health-care.

How does PDMFC act on the POSITION II Project?

The main work areas of PDMFC are short-range communications and Cybersecurity.

https://www.ecsel.eu/projects/position-ii
DEFeND
Data Governance for supporting GDPR
Participate Lean more
Intends to create tools to help organizations to be better prepared to manage eventual leakages of information, manipulation and storage of information and validation of the compliance legal requirements.

How will the Project help to comply with GDPR?

DEFeND focuses on topics such as Data Privacy, User Consent, Anonymization, Data Breach Management, Privacy Assessment, among others. It intends to help organizations to collect, analyze and operationalize different aspects and articles of the GDPR, providing a unique integrated platform for data privacy. The platform will be tested on a pilot scenario on four sectors.

How does PDMFC act on the DEFeND Project?

At PDMFC, we are working on an app that will be part of the platform, to be developed on five case studies. Our focus are matters of Privacy, Security and GDPR.
